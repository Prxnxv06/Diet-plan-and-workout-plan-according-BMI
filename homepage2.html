BMI:

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a crucial tool for assessing your overall health. It calculates your weight in relation to your height and provides an indication of whether you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. Monitoring your BMI is essential because it can help you make informed decisions about your fitness and diet, reducing the risk of obesity-related health issues. Keeping your BMI within a healthy range is a key step in maintaining a happy and active lifestyle.
